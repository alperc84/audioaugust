Recorded, edited mastered by Johannes "Hanni" Schneider for the Inexor game project.
Audioaugust is a collection of sound effects, noises and sound loops with the goal to extend the use of sound in Inexor.
Inexor is a free multi-platform open source game which is a fork from the Cube2: Sauerbraten game.
